About KeigoMaster（敬語マスターとは）

KeigoMaster is an AI-powered assistant designed to help you write professional Japanese — including business emails, chats, and polite expressions (敬語).

Whether you are a foreign professional working in Japan or a Japanese speaker polishing your business communication, KeigoMaster rewrites your message into natural, culturally correct Japanese.

Key Features（主な機能）

  • AI rewriting into perfect business Japanese
  • Soft polite tone or strict business tone
  • Chat-style response generation
  • Supports English → Japanese and Japanese → Japanese
  • Cross-platform support: iOS / Android / Web

Mission（ミッション）

Our mission is to help everyone communicate confidently in Japanese professional environments by providing fast, accurate, and culturally appropriate language support.
